Why OpenRise

Our Vision:
We dream of a labor market without gaps – a system where vacant positions no longer put pressure on existing staff. The motivation for developing and founding our platform lies in societal problems that are felt every day:

  • Long waiting times for health services
  • Traffic disruptions due to permanent construction sites
  • Long waiting times for trade appointments
  • Difficulties in filling key positions in companies
  • Shortages in nursing care
  • Delays in official applications
